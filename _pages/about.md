I am a computer science undergraduate at Hanyang University, interested in autonomous driving, computer vision, and AI systems. My recent work includes research internships at IRCV Lab and AAIS Lab, as well as projects in reinforcement learning, pathology foundation models, 3D reconstruction, and vision-based measurement.

Current focus

- Bachelor student in the Department of Computer Science, Hanyang University.
- Project experience across autonomous driving, computer vision, pathology image preprocessing, and applied AI evaluation.
